Severe weather alert: Brace yourself for heavy rain, snow and dangerous storms this week
A powerful cut-off low is set to strike South Africa from today, with widespread rain, icy temperatures and severe storms that may trigger flash floods, hail damage and snowfall in parts of the country. The South African Weather Service has issued a Level 4 warning for disruptive rain, with provinces including the Free State, North West and KwaZulu-Natal expected to be hit the hardest.
Julia Evans reports that authorities are urging residents to take urgent precautions against waterlogged ground, plummeting temperatures and more than 50mm of rain forecast in some areas.
