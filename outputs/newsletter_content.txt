Dear News24 Reader,

South Africa takes center stage this weekend as it hosts the G20 Leaders’ Summit.

All eyes are on Johannesburg as global leaders gather amid rising tensions sparked by debates over the United States' attendance.

With South Africa's historic role as the first African G20 host being put to the test, News24 will take you inside the two-day summit to bring you live updates.

Our dedicated journalists are on the ground to share exclusive interviews and key developments as the weekend unfolds.

Diplomatic rift: Ramaphosa won’t hand over G20 to a US ‘junior official’, says Presidency

President Cyril Ramaphosa will not hand over the G20 presidency to the United States’ chargé d’affaires, Marc D Dillard, who is expected to lead that country’s delegation at this week’s G20 Leaders’ Summit, says the president’s office.

SA tells US it is displeased at last-minute G20 summit decision

South Africa has informed the US that it is not very pleased at Washington’s 11th-hour decision to attend the G20 at diplomatic level, less than a fortnight after US President Donald Trump vowed a total boycott of the event.

EXPLAINER | The G20 Johannesburg Summit: What are the focus points?

The Group of 20 Leaders’ Summit brings together the heads of state, or their representatives in one place to end the presidency of the host countries term. Its primary goal is as a forum for international economic cooperation.

‘Four-year-old mentality’: US scholar mocks Trump as he hails Africa’s rise at G20

Renowned American economist Professor Jeffrey Sachs – an outspoken critic of US foreign policy – on Thursday compared US President Donald Trump’s decision to snub the G20 leaders’ summit to a toddler having a tantrum.

UPDATE | Change of heart: US warms to Joburg G20 participation amid 11th-hour talks

President Cyril Ramaphosa confirmed on Thursday that the US has changed its mind about participating in the G20 Leaders’ Summit in Johannesburg. He stated that SA was engaging with the US regarding the nature of its request to participate in the meeting “at the 11th hour.”

PODCAST | The Lead: SA’s high stakes G20 and whiplash from Washington

For the first time in Africa’s history, the G20 Summit is happening this weekend in Johannesburg, a city beset by a poisonous urban decay. We catch up with political journalist, Amanda Khoza on the G20 first, and then cross to editor-in-chief Adriaan Basson on the B-20.

G100? No, G-million: Ramaphosa hits back at US jibe about bloated summit

President Cyril Ramaphosa called for debt burdens on African countries to be lowered and a return to a rules-based world order as he addressed the final day of the B20 summit in Sandton. The B20 is the business wing of the G20.
